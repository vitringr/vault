# Geometric Sequence

(Geometric Progression)

Sequence such that each term is obtained by multiplying the previous term by a constant factor.

Examples:

`2, 4, 8, 16, 32, 64...`

`1, 1, 1, 1, 1, 1...`

`3, -9, 27, -81, 243, -729...`
