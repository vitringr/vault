# King's Indian Defense

[[King's Indian]] opening for Black.

Characterized by a kingside [[Fianchetto]] and Knight.

```chesser
fen: rnbq1rk1/ppp1ppbp/3p1np1/8/8/8/PPPPPPPP/RNBQKBNR w KQq - 0 1

```
